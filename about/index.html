About ShadeCast

The Sunglasses Intelligence Engine

1. Our Mission

ShadeCast was built for outdoor athletes, drivers, golfers, and water-sport enthusiasts who need optimal optical clarity. While most people buy sunglasses based purely on aesthetic design, correct eye protection and vision enhancement require understanding lens transmission (VLT), tint filtration spectrums, and polarization layers.

Our goal is to make it easy to find exactly the right sunglasses configurations for your local environment, ensuring maximum comfort and safety under the sun.

2. The Science Behind the Recommendations

ShadeCast maps local real-time coordinate data to analyze live UV indexes and visible solar light levels. Our matching engine then runs calculations factoring in:

* VLT (Visible Light Transmission): The percentage of light that passes through the lens. Category 4 lenses block up to 95% of light for intense alpine snow glare, while Category 1-0 rose and clear lenses boost details in low light or night conditions.

* Color Tint Spectrum Filters: Neutral grey lenses maintain accurate colors under bright driving conditions. Amber and bronze tints filter blue light scatter to enhance depth perception in low sun or foliage-heavy settings (such as disc golf and trail running). Green/G-15 tints offer balanced all-weather contrast.

* Polarization: Horizontal filters cut blinding glare reflection bouncing off roads, water, or car hoods, which is essential for boating, fishing, and driving.

3. Platform Transparency

ShadeCast is built on an open database verified directly against manufacturer technical documentation (including Oakley, Ray-Ban, Maui Jim, Smith, and Goodr). We do not require accounts, logins, or tracking cookies to keep your usage anonymous.
