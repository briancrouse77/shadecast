Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 12, 2026

1. Overview

ShadeCast ("we", "our", or "us") provides a weather-driven sunglasses lens advisor tool. We respect the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy details how we handle information when you interact with our website and application.

2. Data Collection & Privacy

No Personal Data Collection: We do not require account creation, logins, or names. We do not host, store, or sell any personal identifying information.

Geolocation Telemetry: To provide accurate weather recommendations, our site requests access to your device's geographical coordinates locally. This telemetry is processed entirely client-side inside your browser to call the Open-Meteo weather API. Your coordinate details are never stored on our servers.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to store your selected activity preferences, maximum budget filters, and brand parameters locally. This ensures your custom preferences persist across page refreshes for your convenience. No data is shared with third parties from your local storage.

4. Commercial Affiliate Disclosure

ShadeCast is a participant in commercial affiliate marketing advertising programs.

Affiliate Disclaimer: When you click on matched sunglasses cards and purchase items through the links on this website, we may receive a commission. This outbound tracking is handled via affiliate networks including AvantLink, CJ Affiliate, Impact, and ShareASale. These networks place cookies on your browser to track click conversion values and coordinate payouts.

5. External Links

Our service contains links to external retail stores and manufacturers (such as REI, Backcountry, Oakley, Ray-Ban, and Amazon). Once you redirect to these domains, you are governed by their respective privacy policies and purchase agreements.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy statement or our commercial operations, please contact us at support@shadecast.com.
